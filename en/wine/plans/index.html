FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No new charge will occur after your next renewal date, and you keep access until the current period ends.
Is Sakura free?
Login is free, but Sakura itself is a monthly subscription. We may offer a short trial, but the real value is in the paid plan.
Can I use the same account as the sake site?
Yes. Terroir HUB shares one account across sake, shochu, whisky, and wine. Once you log in anywhere, you can use the wine Sakura and the credits are shared.
How can I pay?
We accept major credit cards through Stripe, including Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and JCB.
Can I change plans later?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect from the next renewal date.
